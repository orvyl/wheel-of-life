The Wheel of Life

Self-Growth

Continuous process of learning, improving, and evolving as an individual.

Spirituality

The exploration and connection to something greater than oneself, often involving beliefs about the universe, purpose, and inner peace.

Recreation

The enjoyable and leisurely activities pursued for relaxation, entertainment, and personal fulfillment.

Finances

Your income, expenses, and savings to build a solid foundation for your future financial well-being.

Career

Pursuing your passions and build a successful working life.

Relationships

Bonds you created with others through understanding, support, and care, making life more meaningful together.

Friends

Trusted companions who bring joy and support into our lives as we navigate both good times and challenges together.

Health

Your well-being, encompassing physical, mental, and emotional vitality, achievable through balanced habits and self-care.

Family

The close-knit circle of love and support that makes life meaningful.
